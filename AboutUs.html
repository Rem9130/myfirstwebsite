About Carvings:





We Strive to show our work of Art through Wood Carvings, we produce Religious items from Life size Saints to small sizes, we also do daily life routine carvings such as Children playing, Farmers plowing fields and many more others.




Our shop is Located in the Carving Capital of the Philippines known as Paete,Laguna. As the town livelihood is base on Wood carvings there is also some other things Carvings shows art in such as Paper Mache or Taka in Tagalog, festivities are also filled with our products such us holy week, and Christmas. For that we really show are best Wood Carvings to our Customers.
